FAQ

What is included in the workshop resources?
What is included in the workshop resources?
The resources include step-by-step guides, project plans, and supplemental teaching materials. We also provide a suggested delivery plan with operational checklists to help instructors prepare for and lead hands-on workshops and group discussions.
Do I need prior experience in robotics or programming to use Viam?
Do I need prior experience in robotics or programming to use Viam?
Viam is designed to be accessible to individuals with varying levels of technical experience. Our workshop resources are beginner-friendly and provide clear instructions. However, instructors should have a fundamental understanding of programming and hardware to better facilitate group discussions and aid with troubleshooting.
How can I get additional support to lead a workshop?
How can I get additional support to lead a workshop?
Viam provides operational checklists and troubleshooting guides to confidently host your own workshops. If you need further assistance, we're here to help! Join the dedicated channel #educator-workshops in the Viam Discord community to ask questions, share ideas, and connect with others.
Is there a cost to access these resources?
Is there a cost to access these resources?
The learning materials are available for free. You can access them online and customize them for your needs. Educators and learners must provide their own hardware. However, most workshops are designed to accommodate sharing hardware, and Viam provides suggested alternatives for parts where applicable.
Can I use Viam resources outside of a classroom setting?
Can I use Viam resources outside of a classroom setting?
Absolutely! Viam workshops are suitable for maker spaces, corporate events, and meetups. Additionally, the resources are flexible to fit various learning environments, and in many cases, may be facilitated in a fully virtual environment.
